Official College Basketball GLICKO Ratings

A innovative new approach to NCAA College Basketball rankings
Originally designed as a rating system for chess, ELO has taken over the statistics sports world. Currently, many websites, like Nate Silver's 538, use ELO to rate teams and predict wins for NBA and college basketball; however, this ELO model does not take into account the reliability of ratings, and thus overestimates win probabilities. In order to predict more accurately the outcomes of games, Professor Mark Glickman invented the GLICKO model. Unfortuntaley, this newer, superior model has not caught on, and no major websites currently use GLICKO to predict college basketball games.
