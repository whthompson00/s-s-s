Debate

Throughout all four years of high school, I participated in Public Forum, a debate event in which teams of two argue for and against a current events topic which changes monthly. Debate really had a huge impact on my life as a thinker and as a person. As such, I am passionate about helping underserved students grow their debating skills. This past year, I assisted debaters in the Dominican Republic with research and helps develop their persuasive skills.

In the 2016 debate season, I won the Georgetown University Public Forum Debate Tournament going a perfect 10-0 during the debates. At the tournament, I advocated for and against a carbon tax. I repeated that result in the 2017 season, becoming the first debater to win the tournament in consecutive years. I also finished in the top 30 at the national debate championship in back to back years. These are my only claim to fame in life.
